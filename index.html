Perception for Computers and Robots

Computer Vision section of "Vision & Aerial Robotics Group"

We are a Computer Vision research group within the Technical University of Madrid (UPM). We are very interested in face and body analysis on video (e.g. our SPIGA facial landmarks detector have been one of the best in WFLW benchmark). We are also very interested in computationally efficient Computer Vision mainly applied to image alingment (e.g. we have developed BEBLID and TEBLID two very efficient local image descriptors that can be found in OpenCV).
